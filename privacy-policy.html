WILL App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Simple Summary: WILL is a private accountability platform. We only collect what's necessary for your account and circles to function. We don't share your data with anyone.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and login
  • First and Last Name: Used for identification within your accountability circles
  • Password: Securely stored and encrypted for account access

Circle and Commitment Data

  • Circle Membership: Information about which circles you belong to
  • Will Commitments: Your personal goals and motivations (kept private within your circle)
  • Progress Updates: Your daily progress on active commitments

Video Call Data

  • Camera and Microphone Access: Used only during End Room video sessions
  • Video Calls: Handled by Daily.co (third-party service) - no recordings stored by WILL

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your user account
  • Circle Functionality: Connecting you with your accountability circle members
  • Commitment Tracking: Enabling you to create, track, and complete your goals
  • Video Sessions: Facilitating End Room reflection sessions with your circle

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Within Your Circle

  • Your first name and commitments are visible to your circle members
  • Your "Why" motivations are private and only visible to you
  • Progress updates are shared within your circle for accountability

Third-Party Services

  • Daily.co: Video calling service for End Room sessions (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Hosting: App hosted on Replit with standard security measures

4. Data Security

  • All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • No data is stored on your device beyond standard app functionality

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View your account information through the app settings
  • Update: Modify your personal information at any time
  • Delete: Leave circles or delete your account completely
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active
  • Circle and commitment data is retained for historical reference
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

WILL is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

Email: privacy@willapp.com

App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in account settings
