Header
Title reads "Will Hub". Back button on the left. Two badges centered below: "Active · Day X" in green and the will type (Recurring / Duration / Event) in purple.

Creator's avatar with a 👑 crown indicator. Username and "Creator · X days in" as subtitle. "THE COMMITMENT" label and quoted commitment text below. The "because" / reason field is private — never shown publicly.
3 stat boxes
Days in / success rate in green / joined count with "→" arrow. Tapping joined count opens a list of who has joined with their avatar, username, and join date.
Progress section
Adapts based on will type. Recurring → 7-day week strip. Duration → circular ring (Day X of Y) with calendar. Event → simple Completed / Not yet status card. Label reads "[Creator]'s Week" for Recurring, "THEIR PROGRESS" for Duration, "STATUS" for Event.

Single full-width purple button: "Join Will." This is the only action in the bottom bar. Push lives in the Push card. Chat entry lives in the Hub Chat card. Nothing else belongs in the bottom bar.
