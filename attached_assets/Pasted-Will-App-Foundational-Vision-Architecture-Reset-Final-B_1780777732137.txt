The Vision
Will is a commitment and accountability platform built around one idea: intention must be acted on. The app operates across three levels of human motivation ‚Äî personal growth, social accountability, and community/competition. Every will a user creates lives at one of these levels.
A user's wills are their posts. Their profile is their commitment history. Explore is the feed. Private toggle = hidden from the world. The content is not curated highlights ‚Äî it is real, ongoing, lived commitments.

The Five Will Types
1. Solo Will

Purpose: Personal development, self-growth, personal goals
Who does it: Just the creator
Visibility toggle: Appears on the "What would you like to do?" screen at the very start of creation. Toggle label: "Make this will private", default OFF (open). Subtext: "Private wills won't appear on Explore or your profile"
Default visibility: Open
Joinability: Not joinable
Outside interaction: Viewers can Push and send messages to the creator

2. Team Will ‚Äî I Will

Purpose: Different individual goals, shared accountability between friends
Who does it: Creator + invited members (invite only)
Visibility toggle: Appears on the "What would you like to do?" screen at the very start of creation. Toggle label: "Make this will private", default OFF (open). Subtext: "Private wills won't appear on your profile. Only invited members will see it"
Default visibility: Open
Joinability: Invite only. Strangers cannot join
Outside interaction: Read-only for non-members. No Push, no messages from outside

3. Team Will ‚Äî We Will

Purpose: One shared goal, everyone commits together
Who does it: Creator + invited members (invite only)
Visibility toggle: Same as I Will above
Default visibility: Open
Joinability: Invite only. Strangers cannot join
Outside interaction: Read-only for non-members. No Push, no messages from outside

4. Public Will

Purpose: One goal, open community format. Anyone can join and participate. Meant to inspire ‚Äî someone on day 125 of an ultra marathon with strangers who joined alongside them
Who does it: Creator + any user who chooses to join
Visibility: Always open. No privacy toggle
Joinability: Open to anyone
Outside interaction: Push, messages, Join ‚Äî all available
Design changes: Covered in separate prompt

5. Challenge Will (scaffold only ‚Äî do not build UI yet)

Purpose: Competition, leaderboard, rankings
Visibility: Configurable at creation (open or private)
Design: Needed from scratch ‚Äî future prompt
