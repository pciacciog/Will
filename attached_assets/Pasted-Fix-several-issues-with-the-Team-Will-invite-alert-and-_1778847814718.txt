Fix several issues with the Team Will invite alert and commit flow.

---

CHANGE 1: Simplify the invite alert card

Current: Alert card shows handshake emoji, commitment text, and two 
buttons — "Accept" and "Decline".

Fix:
- Remove the handshake emoji entirely
- Replace "Accept" and "Decline" buttons with a single "View" button
- Tapping "View" navigates to the Team Will Invite detail page
- The Team Will Invite detail page is where the user accepts or declines
- The alert card itself should only serve as a notification entry point, 
  not a decision point

---

CHANGE 2: No stacking alerts — one at a time

Current: Multiple invite alerts stack on top of each other on the 
home screen.

Fix:
- Only ever show ONE alert card on the home screen — the most recent one
- Below the single alert card, if there are additional pending alerts, 
  show a subtle text link:
  "+X more alerts — view all"
  where X is the count of remaining unaddressed alerts
- Tapping "view all" navigates to the notifications / alerts screen
- Never render more than one alert card stacked

---

CHANGE 3: Auto-dismiss alerts when already addressed

Current: If a user addresses an invite through any path other than the 
home screen alert (e.g. via My Wills → Team tab → accepts there), the 
home screen alert remains visible even though it's no longer relevant.

Fix:
- The app must track invite status globally, not per-entry-point
- Whenever an invite is resolved — accepted, declined, or expired — 
  through ANY path in the app, the corresponding home screen alert 
  must be dismissed immediately
- Specifically: after a user completes the full commitment flow for a 
  Team Will (reaches "I'm in"), check for any home screen alert tied 
  to that same Team Will invite and remove it
- This sync should happen on app foreground and on navigation back 
  to the home screen at minimum, ideally in real time

---

CHANGE 4: Final commit screen shows all members including pending

Current: The final screen of the commitment flow (the "I'm in" / 
handshake screen) only shows members who have already fully committed.

Fix:
- Show ALL invited members on the final commit screen, not just 
  committed ones
- Members who have fully committed: show avatar + name normally
- Members who are still pending: show avatar + name + a "Pending" 
  label beneath (muted gray, same style as the pre-start MEMBERS 
  section)
- This gives the joining user full context of who is potentially 
  in this Will with them, matching the experience of the first 
  invite screen
