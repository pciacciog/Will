Design reference — three UI states
The Solo Will dashboard currently has this structure for all Wills:

Nav bar + Active badge
Timeline card
Your Commitment card (what + why)
Daily Check-in button (standalone, outside any card)
Daily Progress card (completed/partial/missed stat boxes + calendar strip)

The redesign keeps the Timeline card and Your Commitment card identical across all three categories. Only the commitment hero treatment, the action button, and the progress section change per category.
